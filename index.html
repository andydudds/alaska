Activities

Jun 15–18 are locked in. June 14 is ours — spa, rest, explore. Jun 13 and 19 are in pencil.

Day 1 — Fixed
Arrival Day
Saturday, June 13
Jan arrives 10:40 AM. Andy arrives 1:33 PM. Drive the Seward Highway to Girdwood (40 min, one of the most scenic drives in North America). Check in at Alyeska. Breathe. Dinner at Two Trees Bistro — just Andy and Mams.
Day 2 — June 14
Nordic Spa + Portage Valley Hike
Spa: 50,000 sq ft · Hike: 2-3 hrs
Morning hike through glacial ponds and valley (moose, bear, eagle sightings possible). Then: hot/cold pools, signature sauna, exfoliation cabin, massages. Two Trees Bistro for food and drinks. Adults only.
Book spa and massages in advance.
Day 3 — June 15
Discover Denali by Plane
Rust's Flying Service · 1:00 PM check-in · 1:30 PM departure · ~3 hrs
Fly over Denali (20,310 ft) — tallest peak in North America. Over the Alaska Range, Ruth Glacier's Great Gorge, Tokositna Glacier, open tundra, braided rivers. Watch for moose, bear, swans. Check in at Rust's Lake Hood Office (4525 Enstrom Circle, Anchorage) 30 min before departure. Sunglasses, camera, light coat. No sandals/heels/dress shoes. Allow 1 hr drive from the hotel — leave by noon. Parking is free but limited at the Rust's office; you'll need to leave your car keys with them.
Day 4 — June 16
Comet to Spencer Heli Hiking & Rafting
Chugach Adventures · 7:45 AM hotel lobby pickup · Private · Full day · 3 adults, 5 children
Helicopter into the Chugach National Forest, landing 2,000 ft up next to Comet Glacier. Hike 3–7 miles downhill to Spencer Glacier Lake for an iceberg rafting tour. End the day on the Alaska Railroad Glacier Discovery Train back to Girdwood (arrives 5:45 PM). Private — just your crew and one guide for the helicopter and hike. Complete the waiver from your confirmation email.
Day 5 — June 17
Glacier Dog Sledding
Alpine Air Alaska · 10:25 AM hotel pickup · 10:55 AM departure · Conf #340510257
Helicopter into the Chugach Mountains. Land on a glacier. Mush with actual Iditarod huskies. Nowhere else on Earth. Dress in warm layers, sunglasses, camera — rain pants and overboots provided. Complete the waiver from your confirmation email.
Day 6 — June 18
26 Glacier Cruise
Phillips Cruises · 5.25 hrs · Check in 11 AM Whittier · Conf #340509096
Leave the hotel by 9:45 AM to make the 10:30 AM tunnel opening to Whittier. Glaciers, humpbacks, orcas, sea otters, puffins — Prince William Sound has one of the world's highest concentrations of tidewater glaciers. Watch tidewater walls calve straight into the sea. Chowder included.
Day 7 — Fixed
Last Morning: Fishing + Farewell
~3-4 hrs · 15 min from Alyeska
On the water by 7 AM. Done by 11. Motorboat up Twenty-Mile River, glacier backdrop, salmon and trout from shore. Just your crew and one guide. Then farewell lunch in Anchorage before everyone scatters.

Intel

Things worth knowing before wheels down.

Rental Car
Picked up at ANC on arrival Jun 13. Returned before departure Jun 19. Compare rental options.
Seward Highway
~40 min ANC to Girdwood. One of the most scenic drives in North America. Turnagain Arm, hanging glaciers, Dall sheep, possible beluga whales. Mammerz gets the window seat.
Denali Day Driving Directions
Mon, June 15 requires a ~1 hr drive to Anchorage. Arlberg Ave down the hill → right onto Alyeska Hwy → Seward Hwy north to Anchorage → O'Malley exit → left onto O'Malley (becomes Minnesota Dr) → International Airport Rd exit → left → right on Postmark → right on Heliport → right on Aircraft Dr. Look for Rust's on the Lake Hood Floatplane dock. Rust's: flyrusts.com · 907-243-1595.
Concierge
Lori Hibbs — lhibbs@alyeskaresort.com — 907-754-2108 (direct) / 907-754-2111 (front desk)
Whittier Tunnel
Thurs, June 18 glacier cruise requires the Anton Anderson Memorial Tunnel to Whittier. One lane, alternating direction, timed openings. Must make the 10:30 AM tunnel opening — leave hotel by 9:45 AM.
Fishing Licenses
Alaska fishing license required for all three. Arrange through your guide or in advance at adfg.state.ak.us.
Waivers
Both Chugach Adventures (Tues, June 16) and Alpine Air Alaska (Wed, June 17) require waivers completed before the trip. Links are in the confirmation emails sent to Jan.
Double Musky Warning
No reservations accepted. Closed Mondays. Arrive at 5 PM open or don't arrive at all.
What Got Cut
Redoubt Bay Bear Viewing (not salmon season, spendy). Veilbreaker Skybridges (not open until July). Private boat charter ($8K — holy shit). Kayaking (needle in a haystack per Benji).
